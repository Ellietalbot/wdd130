Content

Home page

Welcome to Choose Your Adventure! Here we make it easy for you to find adventures that are a perfect fit for you! With trips organized by time, cost, and activity level, you can find the perfect weekend getaway or an adventure you’ll remember forever. We want to make it easy for you to say “cya” to the cares of everyday life and step into a world of wonders!

Trip Previews

Have you ever read a “Choose Your Own Adventure” book and wanted to experience that magical world for yourself? These quick cards can help you take the first step in planning your next big adventure.

Florida: With its white sand beaches and turquoise water Florida has always been a popular vacation spot. You can find many activities to help you sit back and relax, such as visiting the picturesque towns of Seaside and Alys Beach, swimming with manatees or taking a yoga class on the beach. Time: Weekend to a Week Cost: $500-$1000 Activity Level: Low
Greece: Known for its hospitality, great food, history, and deep blue waters, Greece is a great place to visit anytime. You can stroll along its twisting roads, make new friends with the locals, immerse yourself in history, or take a dive in the Mediterranean. You are sure to find something to do that helps you feel at home in Greece. Time: Week or more Cost: $1500-$3000 Activity Level: Medium
Utah: Utah is your perfect adventure destination. It is famous for a landscape that offers anything a modern-day adventurer would want. Red rock formations, excellent ski resorts, beautiful mountains, aspen forests, and turquoise lakes will keep you busy with something new to do every day, all within a short distance from each other. Time: Weekend Cost: $200-$750 Activity Level: High
Iceland: Who doesn’t have “seeing the northern lights” on their bucket list? You can cross that off and find new things that should be on your bucket list in Iceland. Relax and warm up as you take a dip in one of the many geothermal pools that dot Iceland’s landscape. Go hiking or drive along the winding roads as you take in the clean and vividly green Icelandic landscape. Explore black rock beaches, glaciers, or the Arctic for a whale spotting trip. Time: Weekend to a week Cost: $1500-$2000 Activity Level: High
Thailand: Immerse yourself in the rich culture of Thailand. With its flavor-packed food, amazing beaches, open-air markets, lush jungles, and festivals, there is something for everyone in Thailand. This budget-friendly travel destination will not leave you wanting. Thailand will make you feel like you are living your life to its fullest. Time: Week or More Cost: $1500-$2000 Activity Level: High
Morocco: Visit the colorful markets and taste the colorful food of Morocco. Take a camel ride through the Sahara, relax on the golden sand beaches, gaze up at the stars from the desert, and explore the rich culture. Morocco can open your eyes to new ideas and help you to experience a new flavor of life. Time: Week or More Cost: $1000-$2000 Activity Level: Low
